Privacy Policy for Rony AI Facebook App

Last updated: November 23, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Rony AI Facebook App (“the App”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when it automatically creates and publishes content to the Rony Facebook Page using AI.

By using the App in connection with Facebook, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Who We Are

The Rony AI Facebook App is a tool used to automatically generate and publish posts (text and images) to the Rony Facebook Page using AI services such as OpenAI or Microsoft Copilot.

  • Page name: Rony
  • Platform: Facebook Page
  • Contact: your-email@example.com

2. What Information We Collect

The App itself does not directly collect personal information from you outside of what is provided by Facebook or our AI providers.

Depending on how Facebook and AI services work, we may have access to:

  1. Facebook-provided data
    • Basic Page information (Page ID, Page name, followers count)
    • Posts created and published by the App
    • Basic engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares, reach) as provided by Facebook

    This data is used only to operate and improve automated posting.

  2. System and technical data
    • Logs of when an automated post was generated and published
    • Internal error messages or status codes (for troubleshooting)
  3. AI-generated content
    • Text and images generated by AI for the purpose of posting to the Rony Facebook Page

We do not intentionally collect:

  • Individual users’ names
  • Direct messages
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Payment information

Any such information visible through Facebook insights is handled in aggregate and not used to identify specific individuals.

3. How We Use the Information

We use the information described above solely to:

  • Automatically generate and publish posts (text and/or image) to the Rony Facebook Page
  • Monitor whether posts were successfully published
  • Improve or troubleshoot the automation (for example, fixing errors if posting fails)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may interact with the following third-party services:

  1. Facebook (Meta Platforms, Inc.)

    Used for:

    • Managing and publishing posts on the Rony Facebook Page
    • Receiving basic Page insights and metrics

    Your use of Facebook is governed by Facebook’s own Terms and Data Policy.

  2. AI Providers (e.g., OpenAI, Microsoft, or similar)

    Used for:

    • Generating text content (captions, descriptions, hashtags)
    • Generating or describing images

    These services may process the prompt text and content in order to generate the requested output. We do not send them sensitive personal data intentionally.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share or sell your data to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data may be shared only in the following limited situations:

  • Service providers: to the extent needed to operate the automation (e.g., hosting, logging, AI APIs)
  • Legal reasons: if required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request and only to the minimum extent necessary

6. Data Retention

We retain:

  • Logs related to posting activity (for debugging and auditing)
  • AI-generated content (captions, images) that was posted to the Rony Facebook Page

We do not intentionally retain or store personal data of individual Facebook users beyond what is automatically stored by Facebook or our hosting/platform tools.

We keep data only for as long as necessary to operate the App and comply with legal obligations.

Although you may not interact directly with the App, you can:
